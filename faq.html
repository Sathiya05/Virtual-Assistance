Integration & Onboarding

How we transition your chaotic workflows into a streamlined operational engine in under 72 hours.

Our standard integration timeline is 72 business hours. Once your agreement is signed and your retainer is paid, we initiate our secure handover protocol. You will immediately get access to your Client Portal and we will schedule your 60-minute Discovery Dive.

Because we meticulously document your Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) during onboarding, your operations never halt. If your primary partner is unavailable, a fully-briefed, senior-level backup partner will step in seamlessly, ensuring zero disruption to your day.

Security & Confidentiality

How we protect your sensitive data, credentials, and trade secrets.

We operate strictly on a "zero-knowledge" basis. We use 1Password (or your preferred enterprise password manager) to share access. You never send us raw passwords. You grant delegated access via the vault, meaning you can revoke our access with a single click at any time.

Retainers & Financials

Transparent pricing, rollover policies, and billing mechanics.

Neither. We operate exclusively on flat-rate monthly retainers. This ensures predictable billing for you and guarantees your partner's availability. We do not believe in penalizing you (by charging more) when we work efficiently, nor do we want you worrying about a ticking clock.

No, hours do not roll over. Your monthly retainer reserves your partner's guaranteed capacity for that specific month. To ensure our boutique level of service, we cap the number of clients each partner takes, making rollover hours impossible to schedule accurately.

We require a 3-month initial commitment because it takes time to fully integrate into your life and begin yielding maximum ROI. After the first 90 days, your agreement shifts to a flexible month-to-month retainer with a 30-day cancellation notice.

Scope & Boundaries

A premium service is defined by what it *doesn't* do.

No. We are operational and administrative experts. We do not provide outbound sales, telemarketing, cold calling, or direct lead generation services. We *will*, however, meticulously manage your CRM and handle warm client onboarding communications.

While we perform "light bookkeeping" (managing receipts, categorizing expenses, generating invoices), we are not CPAs or attorneys. We cannot file taxes, provide financial audits, or draft legally binding custom contracts from scratch. We act as the liaison *between* you and your specialized legal/financial teams.
